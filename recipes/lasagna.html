Lasagna


Lasgna is a dish made up of noodles and cheese

This recipe has cheese and noodles

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, brown the meat and onion until cooked through.
  4. Add canned tomatoes, herbs, and spices to the skillet.
  5. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  6. Bake for 30-40 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  7. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
  8. Enjoy your delicious lasagna!
